I first started playing with OpenGL about 10 years ago and got quite far in to making a playable game. Then I found out that I was doing everything wrong.

My project was based on OpenGL 1.0 direct rendering and this is not how modern graphics cards work.

I had to go back to the beginning and start again. That's just how it is with technology sometimes.

Life wasn't great at the time. A series of events over the course of the next few years found me losing my job, my physical and mental health, and many of my relationships with people.

We pick ourselves up. We try again. Failure is an opportunity to learn.
